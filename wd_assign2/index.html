Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that's prepared with steamed milk foam. Variations of the drink involve the use of cream instead of milk, using non-dairy milk substitutes and flavoring with cinnamon or chocolate powder.

Long black

A long black is a style of coffee similar to an Americano, but with a stronger aroma and taste. It's made by pouring a double-shot of espresso or ristretto over hot water.

About Us

The Dreamy Café story began in the early 1990s with a humble bakery patisserie in one of Victoria’s suburbs, Glenferrie. The word soon spread and now Dreamy Café is known for offering quality barista made coffee and wholesome food. Over the years we’ve tinkered, tested and perfected our recipes so you can enjoy fresh, delicious, heart warming goodness everyday!
